YOUR ROLE

The E-BEES spread everywhere before our very eyes. The government lets them destroy, invade, kill. And if the government does nothing, we have to fight back. With or without their approval.

At KEB, you can have a variety of different roles. Might it be mass protest or manifesting, to actual sabotage, you fight with all you can. And every method is valid as long as it hurts E-BEES.

In the next sections, you will see what you can do to fight with us, whatever skills you may have.

1. MANIFESTING

If you’re not a tech genius or a risky person, it’s alright. You just need a voice.

As a manifester, you go out months after months, reasoning people, telling the truth.

You may not have drones or a lot of tech knowledge, but you’re a real warrior, and you’re part of the 75% of people that compose our members. Each new voice puts more pressure onto the government.

You go to protests, fairs, conferences. Turn your voice into a weapon. You propagate our ideas. People need to wake up and see the truth.

2. OBSERVING

If you want to do a bit more than manifesting, you could be one of our spies.

You could be an observer. You spot, you track and you report through our APIDETECT network.

If you think people around a hive are in danger, you must also call the authorities.

If you want a more direct contact, join our product team. You go to member’s houses and sell them our anti E-BEES product.

3. HACKING

If you’ve got advanced tech skills, you’re welcome into the Hacking Team.

Your role is to sabotage those hives and bees. Send signals, mess with their code. You crack them open and make them numb. You also build defense devices for people.

And you’re really good at this, hijack a bee. Make it yours. Turn it into a weapon that spreads into their hives.

4. INFILTRATING

Finally, if you do not fear authorities and illegal activities. Join the Infiltration Team.

As the risk of this job is high, you decide whenever and wherever you do this activity. There’s no rule.

As an Infiltrator, you set traps, you destroy them with your bare hands or with weapons.

But if you want to go further, you infiltrate mega corporations and sabotage. Leak data, expose them. Make an impact on the world.
